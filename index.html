Puerto Varas

Puerto Varas is a city in the south of Chile. It's a very famous city for its German architecture and for having two volcanoes side by side. It's also one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country due to the incredible amount of green landscapes. However, it's very cold and it rains for more than half the year.
